About Me

I am a researcher at the Division of Robotics, Perception, and Learning at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, where my work is centered on developing robust localization and mapping capabilities for complex, large-scale environments using consumer-grade LiDARs with minimal human intervention.

My research is focused on directly supporting downstream tasks, enhancing the autonomy and efficiency of robotic systems in real-world scenarios. With over six years of experience in autonomous navigation, I have actively contributed to multiple groundbreaking projects, advancing the field through novel SLAM algorithms, sensor fusion techniques, and control strategies.

My research interests include large-scale SLAM, LiDAR-based perception, sensor fusion for autonomous systems, and life-long mapping solutions. I am passionate about creating practical solutions that bridge the gap between academic research and real-world applications.

Key Areas of Expertise

Localization & Mapping

Developing robust algorithms for large-scale, real-world environments with consumer-grade sensors.

LiDAR-based Perception

Processing point cloud data for accurate scene understanding and environmental modeling.

SLAM Systems

Designing novel approaches for both indoor and outdoor applications with life-long capabilities.

Sensor Fusion

Integrating LiDAR, IMU, cameras, and GNSS for high-accuracy localization and navigation.

Autonomous Navigation

Enabling self-driving systems across different robotic platforms in complex environments.

AI for Robotics

Leveraging deep learning for place recognition, obstacle detection, and decision-making.

Notable Contributions

Autonomous Vehicles

Autonomous Road-Cleaning

Developed algorithms for autonomous road-cleaning vehicles, enhancing efficiency and precision in urban environments.

PLUTO ATV Navigation

Supervised development of fully autonomous ATV research platform at KTH for campus navigation and data collection.

SLAM Innovations

HD-Map Integration

Designed SLAM algorithms that exploit HD-maps to create globally consistent 3D maps for autonomous driving.

Life-long SLAM

Created a novel life-long SLAM solution achieving over 90% recall/precision on KITTI and indoor datasets.

Large-scale Loop Closure

Developed efficient loop closure detection system for large-scale environments with minimal computational cost.

Swarm & UAV Systems

Fixed-wing UAV Swarm

Contributed to a swarm of 119 UAVs demonstrating complex aerial maneuvers and formation control.

Obstacle Avoidance

Implemented safety mechanisms for UAVs operating in dynamic environments with collision avoidance.

Fuzzy Logic Control

Designed precise control systems optimizing robotic decision-making in uncertain environments.
