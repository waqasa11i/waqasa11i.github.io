SLAM, LiDAR, Mapping

Research Projects

Autonomous Navigation System for PLUTO ATV

KTH Royal Institute of Technology

Supervised the development of PLUTO All-Terrain Vehicle as a fully autonomous research platform. The system integrates localization, mapping, perception, path planning, and control for autonomous operation in large-scale outdoor environments.

Successfully tested on KTH campus

Autonomous Street Sweeper

Infore Enviro, Shenzhen

Developed Level 4 autonomous street sweepers for urban environments. Implemented mapping using factor-graph-based approach and localization with Extended State Kalman Filter (ESKF) fusing LiDAR, GPS, and IMU data.

Operational across China

Life-long SLAM using 3D LiDAR

Shanghai Jiaotong University

Proposed a solution to life-long SLAM by representing the global map as rasterized images of local maps with a management system. Implemented bag of visual words method for loop closure achieving over 90% recall/precision.

Published in IEEE Sensors Journal

Fixed-wing UAV Swarm

China Electronics Technology Group

Developed a swarm of 119 small fixed-wing UAVs demonstrating intensive ejection take-off, aerial assembly, multi-target grouping, and formation encirclement. Implemented leader-follower formation with collision avoidance.
